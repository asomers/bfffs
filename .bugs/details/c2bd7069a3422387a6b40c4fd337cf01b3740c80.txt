[details]
If the min_fanout > 2, Tree::range_delete can cause a Node underflow when it
merges two Nodes that have fewer than min_fanout / 2 nodes each.  For
example, if the two Nodes, after the first pass, are left with 1 item each,
then they'll only have 2 items after merging.
